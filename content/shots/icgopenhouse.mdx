# ICG's Open House Instagram Story

Vertical Instagram story design for ICG's Open House event featuring interactive elements and futuristic aesthetics. Dark navy background with glowing person silhouette and call-to-action button.

## Design Features
- "#GoFightWin" hashtag in top left corner
- "Let's Explore ICG!" main headline text
- "Check This Link" instruction text
- Orange/yellow interactive button for link clicks
- Person silhouette with "ICG'S OPEN HOUSE" hoodie
- Glowing hands effect with light orb
- Dual logo placement (Insan Cendekia and ICG logos)
- Vertical 9:16 aspect ratio optimized for Instagram stories
