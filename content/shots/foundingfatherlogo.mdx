# Founding Logo Design

Clean and minimalist logo design for "The Founding Father" merchandise brand. Features a hand-drawn paper plane icon with bold yellow typography on white background.

## Design Elements
- Hand-sketched paper plane illustration with motion lines
- Bold yellow "FOUNDING" typography in modern sans-serif font
- Clean white background for versatility
- Simple two-color design (yellow and black)
- Scalable vector design suitable for merchandise applications
- Represents innovation, progress, and forward movement
- Perfect for apparel, accessories, and brand identity
