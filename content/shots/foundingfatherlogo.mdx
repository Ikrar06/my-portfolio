# The Founding Father Collection

Mobile e-commerce app presentation for Xenavion's "The Founding Father" merchandise collection. Green-themed design with modern UI elements and product showcase.

## Design Features
- "XENAVION PROUDLY PRESENT" branding header
- Mobile app mockup displaying merchandise catalog
- Green grid background with geometric patterns
- Product cards showing t-shirts and accessories
- Historical figure portrait integration
- "ORDER NOW" call-to-action button
- Contact information with Instagram and WhatsApp icons
- QR code for easy app access
