# Eid al-Adha Greeting Design

Islamic-themed greeting poster for Eid al-Adha featuring traditional Middle Eastern design elements. Rich red color palette with desert landscape imagery and celestial motifs.

- Traditional Islamic arch frame with ornate border
- Crescent moon, sun, and stars representing Islamic symbolism
- Desert landscape with palm trees and sand dunes
- "Eid al-Adha" typography in elegant serif font
- "XENAVION" branding at the bottom
- Warm red gradient background with decorative stars
