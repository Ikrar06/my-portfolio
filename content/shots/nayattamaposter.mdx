# Nayattama Art Performance Poster

Dramatic promotional poster for "Nayattama" high school art performance event. Features cinematic design with traditional Indonesian temple setting and theatrical elements.

## Design Elements
- "21 JANUARI 2023 HARUN AR RASYID" event date and director credit
- Giant silhouette figure representing theatrical performance
- Lightning and storm effects for dramatic atmosphere
- Traditional Indonesian temple (Borobudur-style) as cultural backdrop
- Student performer portraits showcasing the cast
- Flying embers creating mystical ambiance
- "XENAVION ARTSHOW" production house credit
- Full cast and crew credits typical of school productions
- Dark, cinematic aesthetic emphasizing the cultural arts theme
